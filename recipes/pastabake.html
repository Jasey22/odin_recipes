Vegetable Pasta Bake

Get the maximum flavour out of all the veg in our pasta bake by pan-frying it first. You can also freeze this for up to three months to enjoy on busier days.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • mozzarella
  • pasta
  • cherry tomatoes
  • garlic

Steps to cook

  1. Cook the pasta following pack instructions, reserving a mugful of the pasta water and draining it 2 mins before the stated time.
  2. Heat 2 tbsp of the oil in a deep, non-stick pan over a low-medium heat, then tip in the aubergine.
  3. Stir well to coat in the oil, frying until softened and deeply golden all over, around 10-15 mins. Remove to a bowl and pour the remaining oil into the pan.
  4. Heat the oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6. Mix in the cooked pasta along with a generous splash of the cooking water.
  5. Season the mixture well to taste and tip into a baking dish. Combine the cheeses with the panko and a large pinch of black pepper, then scatter this over the pasta.
